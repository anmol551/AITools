### Feature Engineering
- **Selected Columns**:
  - Original: `text`, `generated`
  - Engineered: `text_length`, `word_count`, `num_sentences`, `avg_word_length`, `num_unique_words`, `num_uppercase_words`, `num_digits`, `num_punctuation`
- **Details**:
  - `text_length`: Character count of `text` (`len(text)`).
  - `word_count`: Number of words (`len(x.split())`).
  - `num_sentences`: Counts periods, exclamations, and question marks (`x.count('.') + x.count('!') + x.count('?')`).
  - `avg_word_length`: Average characters per word (`sum(len(word) for word in x.split()) / len(x.split()) or 1`).
  - `num_unique_words`: Count of unique lowercase words (`len(set(x.lower().split()))`).
  - `num_uppercase_words`: Count of fully uppercase words (`sum(1 for word in x.split() if word.isupper())`).
  - `num_digits`: Count of digit characters (`sum(char.isdigit() for char in x)`).
  - `num_punctuation`: Count of punctuation characters (`sum(1 for char in x if char in string.punctuation)`).
- **Purpose**: Enhances feature set by quantifying text characteristics, aiding model differentiation between human and AI text.
